Vending Devices

Whether you go for a coin checker, coin changer or card reader – with WMF vending devices, you are always on to a winner! So why not enhance self-service processing and reap the benefits?

Coin Checker

WMF 1100 S, WMF 1200 F, WMF 1200 S, WMF 1500 S, WMF 1500 S+, WMF 5000 S, WMF 5000 S+, WMF 9000 S+, WMF 9000 F

Data:
  • Width 105 mm
  • Height 502 mm
  • Depth 350 mm
Specifications:
  • Connection for 1 WMF coffee machine per coin checker
  • Installation on the right side of the coffee machine
  • Acceptance of coins (5 cents to 2 EUR) and WMF token coins
  • No change return
  • Each drink can be set to a specific price
  • Sales price up to 99

*WMF 1200 F/WMF 1200 S is only compatible with a double-price coin checker, WMF 1200 F only without optional side brewing arm.

Card reader body

WMF 1100 S, WMF 1500 S, WMF 1500 S+, WMF 5000 S, WMF 5000 S+, WMF 9000 S+, WMF 9000 F

Data:
  • Width 130 mm
  • Height 500 mm
  • Depth 350 mm
Specifications:
  • Connection for 1 WMF coffee machine per card reader body
  • Installation on the right side of the coffee machine
  • Suitable for MDB-interface
Important information:
  • A card reader is procured by a customer oneself.
  • For installation forward the card reader and all available information to WMF Germany in Geislingen.
  • Card reader compatibility can be checked by WMF Germany (use MDB-interfaces only).
  • Compatible card reader manufacturer are, e.g.: Girovend, Maas, Wittenborg, Cashcard, Kalisch, Alldata/Intercard, Seitz, Saecocard, Schomäcker. Cash card reader: Thales

Vending machines

for WMF combiNation F

Coin checker
Data:
  • Width 170 mm
  • Height 604 mm
  • Depth 496 mm
Equipment:
  • A WMF combiNation F can be connected
  • This is always installed on the right of the coffee machine
  • Acceptance of coins (5 cents to 2 EUR) and WMF token coins
  • No change given
  • Set to a sales price
Coin changer
Data:
  • Width 170 mm
  • Height 604 mm
  • Depth 496 mm
Equipment:
  • Up to 2 WMF coffee machines can be connected for each coin changer
  • This is always installed on the right of the WMF coffee machine
  • Acceptance of coins (5 cents to 2 EUR) and WMF token coins
  • Change given (based on the difference between set price and coins inserted)
  • Each beverage can be set to a specific price
Card reader housing
Data:
  • Width 170 mm
  • Height 604 mm
  • Depth 496 mm
Equipment:
  • Connection for 1 coffee machine
  • This is always installed on the right of the machine
  • Connection via MDB interface
Important information:
  • A card reader is provided by the customer and sent to the WMF factory in Geislingen for installation
  • Forward all information relating to the card reader to WMF
  • Compatible card reader manufacturers are, e.g.: Girovend, Maas, Wittenborg, Cashcard, Kalisch, Alldata / Intercard, Seitz, Saecocard, Schomäcker. Money card reader: Thales
  • WMF checks the card reader beforehand for compatibility on request (only MDB readers are suitable) and then installs it into the housing.
