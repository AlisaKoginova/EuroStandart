WMF Milk systems

With the WMF milk systems, you can prepare professional coffee creations in a flash, without any prior experience!

Basic Milk for classic coffees and Easy Milk for warm and cold milk-mixed drink.

Our new Dynamic Milk system enables the fully automatic preparation of hot and cold milk and milk foam beverages. It offers an even wider range of milk foam consistencies: liquid, solid, creamy, fluffy, ...

WMF Basic Milk

The Basic Milk system is the perfect choice for all classic coffees such as cappuccinos, latte macchiatos and lattes, which are prepared with hot milk and hot milk foam. Basic Milk is available for all WMF bean to cup machines.

WMF Easy Milk

Classic coffees and countless cold milk-mixed drinks can be prepared with the Easy Milk system. The system is available for the WMF 1200 S, WMF 1500 S, WMF 1500 S+, WMF 5000 S and WMF 5000 S+.

WMF Dynamic Milk

The Dynamic Milk system enables the fully automatic preparation of hot and cold milk and milk foam beverages. The Dynamic Milk system offers an even wider range of milk foam consistencies. The Dynamic Milk system is available for the WMF 1500 S, WMF 1500 S+, WMF 5000 S, WMF 5000 S+ and WMF 9000 S+.

Cooler with milk temperature sensor:

The temperature of the milk is a key factor in the quality of the milk foam. An optional sensor in the cooler monitors the milk temperature and indicates it on the graphic display.

The WMF steam systems Basic Steam, Easy Steam and Auto Steam are the perfect complementary partners for the milk systems, providing a user-friendly, practical way of making a huge range of beverages.
