NEW! Center Cooler

The ideal milk cooling unit for single or dual use.

For all specialty machines except Basic Milk machines and WMF 1100 S.

Item number: 03.9022.6041

Data:
  • Capacity: 10 litres
  • External dimensions (width / height / depth): 261 / 530.5 / 469 mm
  • Milk hose routing: At the right and left side possible
  • Sensors: Empty message and temperature monitoring
  • Coolant: R600a
  • Empty weight approx. 16 kg
  • Lockable: Yes
  • Removable seal: Yes

The new WMF Center Cooler is an indispensable companion in modern milk management.
Equipped with a container for up to 10 litres of milk, this all-rounder supplies two fully automatic coffee machines at the same time. This is healthy for the investment budget and also saves space on the counter. Thanks to integrated monitoring functions for temperature fluctuations and fill levels, the Center Cooler makes everyday operations noticeably easier.

The sensor system functions without interruption even if one of the two machines are switched off. Thanks to the new generation design that is perfectly coordinated with the WMF coffee machines, the Center Cooler and Syrup Station are the perfect pair, giving “coffee hubs” an appealingly stylish look.
