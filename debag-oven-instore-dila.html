DILA

The ingeniously simple in-store baking system.

Send request (PDF)

The master of efficiency: quick to heat up, uses minimal energy

DILA is the ingeniously simple in-store baking system with a modular layout. Depending on the capacity and assortment of baked goods, the DILA can be configured to match your individual requirements – from the final baking to the professional baking system. Have a look at our short movie: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f1TshwmDx4s. In addition to that, there is a variety of equipment options available, such as a loading system with easy-to-operate TOUCH control and the fully automated e.CLEAN SYSTEM® cleaning system.

Moreover, you can choose between radial or high-performance steam injection. The FilialNet oven connection software can also be integrated if required. This software makes it possible to control all the connected ovens centrally and maintain them remotely. The whole baking process is documented and straightforward to analyse. This gives you all baking processes at a glance – at any time, from anywhere!

Quick loading

The DEBAG oven loading systems make it much easier to insert and remove trays. The loading trolley for the DILA is compatible with the DEBAG warming cabinet and therefore allows baked goods and snacks to be conveniently transported.

The simplest method of cleaning

Our resource-friendly cleaning systems impress with their low energy and water usage. e.CLEAN signifies improved cleaning power and a decreased workload.

Wide range of options

Whether as a DILA DUAL (oven with 2 glass front panels) or in combination with the HELIOS rack oven - the ingeniously simple DILA module in-store baking system gives you maximum flexibility and is, moreover, available in a variety of different design options.
