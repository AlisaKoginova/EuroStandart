Coffee Station 58 / Coffee Station 58+

The agile solution for independent coffee making.

For WMF 1100 S.

Item number Coffee Station 58: 03.9494.1001

Item number Coffee Station 58+: 03.9494.1101

Data:
  • External dimensions (width / height / depth): 580 / 900 / 600 mm
  • 1 inlaid floor: All 32 mm holes available to move the inlaid floor (only with the Coffee Station 58)
  • Table and base plate with a cable passage: Ø 80 mm
  • 2 double swivel castors: Ø 50 mm
  • 2 double swivel castors with lock: Ø 50 mm
  • Load capacity: Max. 150 kg
  • Empty weight: Approx. 33 kg
  • Colour: Anthracite

Found the perfect machine but there’s no room in the conference room, kitchen or office? Coffee Station 58 from WMF provides a very clever solution. With a compact table width of 58 centimetres, an optional technical group and integrated grounds container, the stationary furniture unit offers sufcient space for the coffee machine plus add-on equipment. Operating independent from the water feed and drain, users can position the station anywhere in the room. Cups, cutlery and accessories can also be easily and safely stowed in the coffee station thanks to the incorporated shelf.
