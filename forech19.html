FORECH 2019

25 — 27 September 2019

International Expoforum of Restaurant, Hotel Business and Cleaning

International Expoforum of Restaurant, Hotel Business and Cleaning FoReCH is the main professional event of the HoReCa sector in Ukraine. Every year FoReCH brings together the industry leaders. The exhibition is a reliable communication platform for making deals and signing contracts. It also allows evaluating the prospects of the development of the hospitality sector.

The hospitality industry is now at the stage of reformatting, however, according to the participants of the forum, the HoReCa market continues to develop and the competitive struggle becomes severe. At such periods, existing customers and the services quality are relied on, therefore every restaurateur and hotelier is looking for new solutions to optimize their business. Reduction of consumers’ possibilities leads to more scrupulous selection of HoReCa establishments and a special demands of a potential client. Nevertheless, restaurateurs and hoteliers are optimistic about the future, trying to improve their service for Ukrainians and foreign guests every day.
